关于 我

I chose my favorite city Chengdu to start my undergraduate study but I had to compromise with my parents on major selection. Accordingly, I entered the study of Auditing instead of communication, my original targeted undergraduate major. With firm belief in “Only by doing what you are doing well can you have the confidence to choose what you want to do”, I excelled many others in the study of Auditing in a highly competitive academic atmosphere, ranking Top 3 out of 100 in Xihua College and qualifying me as a regular winner of national scholarship. But my interest and efforts in media and communication never wane. I tried to seize as many chances as possible to engage in what I’m really interested in, like Media Technology Specialist, Acquisition and Cataloging Journalist, Vice Minister of the Publicity Department, Operator of We-Media “@ Chengdu Free Walker”, and User Operation Intern of Market Channel Department of Sina Weibo. When preparing for the Unified National Graduate Entrance Examination, I undoubtedly chose communication as my master’s degree major, and self-studied communication, advertising, and marketing. I repeatedly read the book Marketing Management for five to six times, developed a systematic knowledge of it, and further confirmed my real passion for communication. I believe that my dedicated preparation and hard work will finally be paid off. Now, as a postgraduate student in East China Normal University (ECNU) ,I am studying the major of Publishing in School of Communication, a momentous step for entering my beloved field.
